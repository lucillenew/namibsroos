About Namibsroos Villa

Villa Description

Situated in the charming coastal town of Hentiesbaai, Namibsroos Villa offers front-row seats to the mesmerizing Atlantic Ocean. The villa's prime location on the Skeleton Coast provides easy access to the region's famous fishing spots, desert adventures, and scenic drives. Just minutes away from local amenities, restaurants, and attractions.

Namibsroos is a luxury villa located on the serene Skeleton Coast, offering 4 spacious bedrooms, a modern kitchen, and a private deck with panoramic ocean views.


  • Four bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms
  • Fully equipped modern kitchen
  • Dining area with dining table and chairs
  • Large living area with bathroom, patio and indoor BBQ/braai area
  • Bon fireplace outside with seating

Location & Activities

Hentiesbaai is a small coastal town in Namibia, known for its scenic beauty, rich history, and outdoor recreational activities. Here are some things to do in Hentiesbaai:

Fishing

Experience world-class fishing along the Skeleton Coast, known for its abundant marine life and excellent angling opportunities.

  • Deep sea fishing
  • Shore angling
  • Local fishing guides

Skeleton Coast National Park

Explore the mesmerizing Skeleton Coast National Park, home to unique wildlife and breathtaking landscapes.

  • Dramatic landscapes
  • Unique wildlife
  • Historic shipwrecks

Hentiesbaai Museum

Immerse yourself in the history and culture of Hentiesbaai, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, which features exhibits of the areas fishing industry, shipwrecks and more.

  • Exhibits of the fishing industry
  • Shipwreck tours
  • Areas history and culture

Stargazing

Due to the town's remote location, Hentiesbaai offers incredible stargazing opportunities on clear nights.

  • Clear skies
  • Stargazing
